About Us

Star MD Billing - Your Billing Solution for a Healthier Practice.

For over 2 decades now, we have been a trusted partner in practice prosperity, providing complete medical billing services to healthcare providers nationwide. Our team of experts actively manages your entire revenue cycle to get you reimbursed faster. We tackle aging claims, follow up on all your submissions, and leverage the latest technologies to prevent denials and boost your bottom line. You can count on us to maximize collections so you can focus on delivering quality care.

  • Specialized Billing For Maximum Reimbursement

    Our dedicated billing team gets you paid quickly by optimizing your revenue cycle from start to finish, maximizing your practice's earnings

  • Simplified Reporting For Complete Transparency

    Access real-time dashboard reports to track payments and identify revenue opportunities, ensuring complete transparency and informed decision-making for your practice.
