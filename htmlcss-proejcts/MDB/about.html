about us

Transforming Billing Complexities into Financial Success

At Star MD Billing, our dedicated team of medical billing experts works tirelessly to maximize your collections so you can get back to focusing on providing quality care. We are passionate about leveraging our extensive experience and the latest technologies to optimize every aspect of your revenue cycle.

  • Maximized Collections

    Our specialized team boosts your revenue by optimizing your entire billing and collections process.

  • Complete Revenue Cycle Insight

    Access real-time dashboard reporting for increased visibility into payments and denials.

Our specialists will verify eligibility, code claims accurately, handle error-free submission and follow-up, appeal denials, and pursue unpaid claims. We offer complete visibility into your revenue cycle through real-time dashboard reporting. With our patient-first values, cutting-edge tools, and precision execution, we ensure minimal denials, faster payments, and increased revenue. Partner with us for reliable medical billing services delivered with a personal touch.

Our Vision

Our vision is to build long-term, trusted partnerships with healthcare providers by becoming an indispensable extension of their business operations. We aim to continually redefine the next generation of medical billing services and technologies to drive improved efficiency, increased collections, and greater profitability for our clients. Through a patient-first philosophy and an unwavering commitment to value, transparency, and executional excellence, we will help our clients navigate industry changes, achieve financial goals, and remain leaders in healthcare.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver patient-centric medical billing services that get our clients paid faster and easier. We are committed to leveraging our expertise to provide innovative solutions that reduce administrative burdens and empower healthcare providers to focus on delivering quality care. Everything we do is driven by our passion for optimizing our clients’ revenue cycles for financial growth and stability. Our team works diligently to maximize reimbursement while minimizing costs through accuracy, timeliness, and relentlessness in pursuing every billable dollar.
